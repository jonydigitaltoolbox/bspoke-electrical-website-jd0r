Our Story

Daniel Spokane founded Bspoke Electrical with a vision to provide personalized, high-quality electrical services for families and businesses in Montreal. His dedication to craftsmanship and safety stems from years of experience in the industry, ensuring that every project reflects his commitment to excellence.
